About Me

Hello! My name is Audra, and I am a digital artist and a fashion lover. My art syle aligns with semi-realism but it also looks animated as well. I am currently in my 3rd semester of college and I am working towards my associates degree in art & design. I have always been interested in fashion and art, so I plan to combine the two and pursue a career in graphic design, with the intent of aligning it with fashion. In my free time, I enjoy creating digital art, exploring new fashion trends,and social activities with friends. I am excited to continue learning and growing as a web designer and artist!
